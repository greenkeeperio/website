Greenkeeper Style Guide

These are the design guidelines and resources for Greenkeeper.

Basics

Colours

Greenkeeper has two basic colours, green for the hosted SaaS product, blue for the self-hosted enterprise product.

  • #00C775Use for the brand mark and logo type, emphasis, box backgrounds, or links.
  • #6495EDUse for the brand mark and logo type, emphasis, box backgrounds, or links, but only in the enterprise context.
  • #656765Standard text colour for pararaphs.
  • #4c4d4cEmphasised text in pararaphs.
  • #000000For headlines.
  • #FFFFFFOur standard background colour is plain white.

Fonts

Our headline and logotype font is Futura. For headlines on the website we use Futura PT in 500 weight from TypeKit. The body text font stack is Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif, and for monospaced type, such as in code examples, we simply rely on font-family: monospaced;.
